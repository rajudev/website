hillhacks 2026 will take place at Bir, Himachal Pradesh, India.The venue is called "The Bagicha". Nearest Bus Stop is "Chougan Chowk".

From Chougan Chowk, you have to take a right towards the location. Although walking is possible, taking a taxi from Chougan Chowk is recommended.

For more details on how to reach Bir itself checkout [https://hillhacks.in/travel/](https://hillhacks.in/travel/)

### Food & Accommodation

Custom food options are also available and it can be requested with Jubeesh. Charged separately.
If the kitchen is available, you can cook.
